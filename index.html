Services

We provide audits & assurance, taxation & compliance, accounting (book-keeping services).

Audits & Assurance

Audit services provided by us includes:

  • Statutory Audit: Audit of Books of Accounts as per (Companies Act, 2013)
  • Income Tax Audit: Audit of Books of Accounts as per (Income Tax Act, 1961)
  • VAT Audit: Audit of Books of Accounts as per (Maharashtra Value Added Tax Act, 2002)
  • Internal Audit: Independent, objective assurance and consulting activity designed to add value and improve an organization's
    • operations.
  • System Audit: Independent assurance check on system of the organization.
  • Stock Audit: Verification of the quantities and condition of items held in an inventory or warehouse
  • Management & Operational Audit: systematic review of effectiveness, efficiency and economy of operation.
  • Transfer Pricing Audit: Audit u/s.92 of the Income Tax Act, 1961 Transaction Verification
